Rules & Regulations

  • The theme for the performance will be provided 3 weeks prior to the event.
  • The choreography must be centred entirely around the given theme.
  • Teams must demonstrate proficiency and skill in both eastern and western dance forms.
  • Any form of vulgarity is strictly prohibited and will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • All required music tracks must be submitted in the provided drive link by 28th July.
  • Teams should also bring their tracks on a pendrive on the day of the performance.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
