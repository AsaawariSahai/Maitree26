Crescendo

5+2 Participants Min. 3 Instrumentalists 5+2 Minutes

Rules & Regulations

  • Each team must include a minimum of 3 instrumentalists.
  • A musical genre and a song from a different genre will be assigned to each team via a lottery system 3 weeks prior to the event.
  • The performance must be executed entirely within the assigned musical genre.
  • Participants must interpret the assigned song into their given genre and include it in the performance for a minimum duration of 1.5 minutes.
  • The use of vernacular languages is permitted.
  • Pre-recorded music or background tracks shall not be allowed.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Judging Criteria

01

Genre Adaptation & Creativity

02

Musical Skill & Technical Proficiency

03

Stage Presence & Team Synchronization

04

Interpretation of Assigned Song

05

Fusion of Musical Elements & Instruments
