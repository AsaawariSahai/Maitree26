Fashion Show

Metamorphosis

8+2 Participants 6+2 Models 2 Designers 7+2 Minutes

Rules & Regulations

  • The theme will be announced 3 weeks prior to the event.
  • Each outfit must be original and created by the designated designers.
  • Three copies of a physical brochure featuring all outfits must be submitted to the judges before the performance on the day of the event.
  • The brochure must indicate the team's interpretation of the theme, detailed descriptions of each outfit, and highlight elements created by the designers.
  • A background presentation or video must also be created as a form of the brochure.
  • Music tracks with appropriate lyrics must be used.
  • All required music tracks and presentations/videos must be submitted in the provided drive link by 28th July.
  • Teams should also bring their tracks and presentations/videos on a pendrive on the day of the performance.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Fusion Music

Crescendo

5+2 Participants Min. 3 Instrumentalists 5+2 Minutes

Rules & Regulations

  • Each team must include a minimum of 3 instrumentalists.
  • A musical genre and a song from a different genre will be assigned to each team via a lottery system 3 weeks prior to the event.
  • The performance must be executed entirely within the assigned musical genre.
  • Participants must interpret the assigned song into their given genre and include it in the performance for a minimum duration of 1.5 minutes.
  • The use of vernacular languages is permitted.
  • Pre-recorded music or background tracks shall not be allowed.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Non-Fire Cooking

Whisked Away

2 Participants 2 Hours 3 Servings per item

Rules & Regulations

  • A global cuisine will be provided to each house using a lottery 3 weeks prior to the event.
  • Every participating house must create a complete three-course presentation consisting of a main dish, a beverage, and a dessert.
  • A secret component will be revealed and provided to each house on the day of the competition. This mystery item must be seamlessly incorporated into all three menu requirements.
  • Teams must prepare and present exactly 3 individual servings of each required menu item for judging.
  • All food must be strictly vegetarian.
  • Participants are responsible for bringing all necessary cooking utensils, materials, and plating dinnerware.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Drama & Playwrighting

Curtain Call

6–8 Participants 4–6 Actors 2 Script Writers 2 Rounds

General Rules

  • Background music, PowerPoint presentations, and audio-visual aids are permitted for both rounds.
  • Any required music tracks must be brought by the team on the day of the performance on a pendrive.
  • Houses are permitted to use their own props in both rounds.
  • Use of vernacular language must be kept to a minimum.
  • The use of abusive language, political references, or religious references is strictly prohibited and will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Art & Creative Writing

Stroke'd

2 Participants 3 Rounds 30 min per round Elimination format

Rules & Regulations

  • There will be a total of 3 rounds. This is an elimination-based event — a house must qualify at the end of each round to progress to the next.
  • Both members must work collaboratively on a single artwork for each round. Individual or divided submissions will not be accepted.
  • The theme for each round will be revealed exactly 5 minutes before the round begins.
  • Each round is designed to test the participants' skills and versatility in different art forms (e.g., canvas painting, sketching, digital art).
  • Participants must bring their own materials and devices. Devices must have digital art applications downloaded and ready.
  • Further details will be provided on the day of the event.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Videography & Editing

Cue!

2+2 Participants 2 Videographers 2 Editors 2-min Final Video

Rules & Regulations

  • Each house must submit a single video showcasing and capturing the events of Maitree.
  • All footage and clips used must be strictly restricted to the team's own house events and participants.
  • Creative interpretation and narrative storytelling of the day's events are permitted and encouraged.
  • Filming and shooting must be conducted live on the day of the event.
  • A maximum of 30 seconds of pre-recorded footage from prior days may be included in the final cut.
  • Usage of AI is prohibited and will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Participants must bring their own devices.
  • The final edited video must be submitted in the provided drive link before 1:20 p.m. on the day of the event.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
